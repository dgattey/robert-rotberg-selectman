Robert I. Rotberg

Candidate for Selectman

Lexington is a well-run town. To build firmly upon the sensible and sensitive governance momentum the past Board of Selectman have fostered, Lexington needs someone with executive and fiscal know-how and a wealth of relevant experience both in and outside the Town.

A long-time Town Meeting member, a former member of the School Committee, and a former university and college administrator, Robert can make the necessary tough decisions that will continue to define Lexington as a community of excellence and innovation.

Robert I. Rotberg

A Strong Leader

Deb Mauger was a strong and able chair of the Board of Selectman. To succeed her, we need someone to strengthen many of our excellent policies, maintain the high quality of our schools and our services, spend more money on roads and other infrastructural needs, make sure that all of our town citizens are served equally well, closely examine and bolster our industrial and commercial base, continue our strict budget policies, and become more “green” and more environmentally sensitive. We will be tested severely by a number of costly capital needs – new schools, new police headquarters, and a new fire station. Given Robert's experience and background, he can help make those tough choices, the better to keep Lexington excellent.
